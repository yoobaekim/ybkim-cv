    • Biograpical Sketch

    Yoobae Kim is the Emeritus Professor in Economics at SungKyunKwan University. He served as a Professor for 30 years, Senior Advisor to the President, and the Minister of Welfare and Patriot, the president of human Resource Center. Here is a log with selected photos from working with Korean Government. He had also worked for the International Agencies such as ILO, World Skills International(WSI).
    Yoobae received his Bachelor's degree from Yonsei University and his Economics Ph.D. from the University of Illinois at U-C. His research interests span many aspects of Arts & Sciences: He received MA degree at the University of Texas at Dallas in 2019. Here is a log with selected photos from my art studio
